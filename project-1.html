Explore Outdoors

Explore Outdoors is a group project done with UCF's Coding Bootcamp. Explore Outdoors is your one stop website to find the perfect destination for your next vacation when you can't decide where to go. Take our 12 question test and find the perfect destination based on six beautiful places we hand picked for you!
